Medical Diagnosis Prediction

Problem Description: The goal is to determine whether a patient is likely to have a specific disease based on symptoms and medical test results. Early detection is critical to improving treatment outcomes.

Type of Machine Learning: Supervised Learning (Classification)

Input Data:

  • Patient age
  • Blood pressure
  • Cholesterol levels
  • Medical history
  • Symptoms reported

Expected Output: A classification result indicating whether the patient is at risk (Positive/Negative diagnosis).

Real-world Relevance: This type of model helps healthcare professionals make faster and more accurate decisions, reducing diagnostic errors and improving patient care.
