Student Performance Prediction

Problem Description: The objective is to predict a student’s academic performance based on study habits and academic history. Many institutions struggle to identify students at risk of failure in time.

Type of Machine Learning: Supervised Learning (Regression)

Input Data:

  • Grade Point Average (GPA)
  • Number of enrolled courses
  • Study hours per week
  • Number of failed subjects

Expected Output: A predicted performance score or risk level (High or Low academic risk).

Real-world Relevance: Educational institutions can use this model to identify at-risk students early and provide academic support, improving retention rates and student success.
