Welcome to Machine Learning

Within this broad ecosystem, linear regression stands as one of the fundamental pillars and the most common entry point to supervised learning, as it allows for modeling the mathematical relationship between a variable of interest and the factors that influence it. While machine learning in general can utilize complex structures such as neural networks, linear regression seeks the “best-fit line” to explain how a dependent variable (such as academic performance or the price of an asset) changes in response to variations in one or more independent variables.
