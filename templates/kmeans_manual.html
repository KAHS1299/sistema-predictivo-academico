Machine Learning No Supervisado

The objective of this manual simulation is to understand the inner workings of the K-Means algorithm by grouping a dataset of 100 records into K=3 clusters. Unlike supervised learning, where the output is known, this exercise demonstrates how the algorithm identifies natural groupings based on the proximity of data points to calculated centroids. The goal is to minimize the intra-cluster variance through iterative refinement. Ejemplo: clustering (K-Means).
