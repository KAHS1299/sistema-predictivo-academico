Chatbot for Customer Support

Problem Description: The goal is to automatically respond to user inquiries in real time, reducing the need for human intervention in repetitive customer service tasks.

Type of Machine Learning: Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Input Data:

  • User text messages
  • Frequently asked questions database
  • Conversation history

Expected Output: Automated responses that simulate human conversation and provide useful information.

Real-world Relevance: Chatbots are widely used in e-commerce, banking, and customer service platforms to improve response time, reduce costs, and enhance user experience.
